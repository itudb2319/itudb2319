Welcome to OnlyF1s!



OnlyF1s is a fandom F1 website created for the ITU BLG317E course, containing data from the years 1950 to 2023.

You can access driver, constructor, circuit, and season information from the top menu.

Moreover, you can access the other tables (Driver Standings, Lap Times etc.) from the 'Others' section.

Additionally, you can access the mini quizzes we've prepared about F1 from the 'Quizzes' section and test your knowledge of F1.

Furthermore, you can have fun and measure your reflex skills with the 'Blink Game' we've created.

Lastly, you can access special rankings created for you through the 'Rankings' section.

For your feedback and suggestions, you can reach us through the 'Contact Us' page located in the footer.
